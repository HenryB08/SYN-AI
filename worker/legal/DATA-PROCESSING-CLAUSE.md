## Data Processing

**1. Roles.** In providing the SYN Growth chat widget and related services (the "Services"),
Syntrex LLC ("Syntrex") acts as a **processor** on behalf of the Client, who is the **controller**
of the personal data of the Client's own end customers ("End-Customer Data") collected through the
Services. The Client decides why and how that data is collected; Syntrex processes it only to
provide the Services.

**2. Scope of processing.** Syntrex processes End-Customer Data only:
(a) to operate the chat assistant, store conversations, capture contacts, record consent, and enable
follow-up on the Client's behalf; and
(b) on the Client's documented instructions, including the configuration the Client sets (brand
profile, consent wording, privacy-policy link). Syntrex does **not** use End-Customer Data for its
own purposes and does **not** sell it.

**3. What is processed.** End-customer messages to the assistant; contact details the end customer
shares or submits (name, email, phone); consent choices and the exact wording shown, with
timestamps; and limited technical data (approximate IP, user-agent) recorded with a consent action
as audit evidence.

**4. Consent.** The Client is responsible for the lawfulness of collecting End-Customer Data and for
the accuracy of the consent language presented in the widget and in the Client's privacy policy.
Syntrex provides the **mechanism**: an unticked-by-default consent checkbox, an append-only consent
audit trail that records the exact language shown, SMS STOP/UNSUBSCRIBE/QUIT handling, a no-login
email unsubscribe link, and manual withdrawal — and Syntrex will not queue follow-up to a channel
after consent for it has been withdrawn. The Client is responsible for any messaging-program
registration (e.g. A2P/10DLC) and for ensuring its actual program terms match the consent wording.

**5. Confidentiality & security.** Syntrex keeps End-Customer Data confidential and maintains
reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risk. [Attach or reference the
specific security-measures schedule.]

**6. Sub-processors.** Syntrex may use sub-processors (e.g. its hosting provider and the SMS/email
delivery providers) to provide the Services, and remains responsible for their performance under
this clause. [List current sub-processors and the notice mechanism for changes.]

**7. Data-subject requests.** Syntrex provides the Client tools to respond to end-customer requests:
export of all data held about a contact, and deletion of a contact and their conversations and
messages while retaining anonymized metrics and consent records. Where an end customer contacts
Syntrex directly, Syntrex will refer them to the Client and assist the Client in responding.

**8. Deletion & retention.** On an end customer's erasure request (or on the Client's instruction),
the contact record, conversations, and messages are deleted; anonymized event metrics and consent
records (with direct identifiers removed) are retained as permitted by law to preserve reporting
integrity and proof of consent. On termination of the Services, Syntrex will delete or return
End-Customer Data on the Client's instruction, subject to retention required by law.
