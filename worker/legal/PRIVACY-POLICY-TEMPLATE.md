# Privacy Policy — TEMPLATE

> **Template, not legal advice.** This is a fill-in template provided by Syntrex LLC for use by
> businesses that deploy the SYN chat widget. It is **not** a lawyer-drafted, jurisdiction-specific
> privacy policy. The business (the data controller) is responsible for completing it, confirming it
> is accurate for how they actually operate, and having it reviewed by qualified legal counsel before
> publishing. Do not publish with any `[MANDATORY]` placeholder unfilled.
>
> A live, auto-filled version of the visitor-facing essentials is served at
> `https://<syn-growth-host>/w/privacy?k=<install_key>`. This document is the fuller canonical
> template; the client should host their own complete policy and set `config.privacy_policy_url` on
> the install so the widget links to it instead of the built-in page.

## [CONTROLLER_LEGAL_NAME] — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: [DATE]**

### Who we are
[CONTROLLER_LEGAL_NAME] ("we", "us", the "business") operates [BRAND_NAME] and is the **data
controller** for personal data collected through the chat assistant on our website. The chat
assistant is operated on our behalf by **Syntrex LLC** as our **data processor** — Syntrex stores and
processes the data only on our instructions and does not use it for its own purposes.

Contact for privacy matters: [PRIVACY_CONTACT_EMAIL] · [BUSINESS_ADDRESS] · [DPO_CONTACT]

### What we collect
- The messages you type into the chat assistant.
- Contact details you share in the chat or submit through the contact form — your name, email
  address, and phone number.
- Your consent choices: whether you agreed to SMS and/or email follow-up, the **exact wording you
  were shown**, and the date and time — kept as an audit record.
- Limited technical data recorded alongside a consent action (approximate IP address and browser
  user-agent) for the sole purpose of evidencing that consent.

### Why we collect it (purpose and basis)
- To respond to your questions and inquiry. *(Basis: to take steps at your request / our legitimate
  interest in answering you.)*
- To follow up about your inquiry by email or, where you have opted in, by SMS.
  *(Basis: your consent for SMS; your consent or our legitimate interest for email — see below.)*
- To keep a record that consent was given or withdrawn. *(Basis: our legal obligation / legitimate
  interest in demonstrating compliance.)*

We do **not** sell your personal data.

### SMS messages
We will only send you text messages if you tick the consent box and provide a mobile number.
Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. **Reply STOP to any message to opt out
at any time** (UNSUBSCRIBE and QUIT also work). Reply HELP for help.

### Email follow-up
If you provide an email address, we may email you about your inquiry. Every follow-up email contains
a one-click unsubscribe link that stops further emails without requiring you to log in.

### How long we keep it
We keep inquiry data only as long as needed to handle your inquiry[, and no longer than
[RETENTION_PERIOD]]. We retain consent records (including opt-outs) as long as needed to evidence
compliance.

### Your rights
Depending on your location you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data,
to withdraw consent, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these,
contact us at [PRIVACY_CONTACT_EMAIL]. We will respond within the time required by applicable law.

You can:
- **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Delete** — request erasure of your contact record and conversations (we retain anonymized
  metrics and consent records as permitted by law).
- **Withdraw consent** — reply STOP to a text, use the unsubscribe link in an email, or contact us.

### Sharing
We share your data with Syntrex LLC (our processor, to run the assistant) and with the service
providers Syntrex uses to deliver messages (e.g. an SMS/email provider). We do not otherwise share
it except as required by law.

### Changes
We may update this policy; the "last updated" date shows when. Material changes will be posted here.

### Governing law
This policy is governed by the laws of [GOVERNING_LAW].
